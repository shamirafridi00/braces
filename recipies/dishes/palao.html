Chicken Pulao

Description

Pulao ek khushbudar chawal ki dish hai jo Biryani se halki hoti hai. Ise gosht ki yakhni (broth) mein pakaya jata hai, jis se chawalon mein ek anokha zaiqa ata hai. Yeh dawat aur aam dinon, dono ke liye ek behtareen intekhab hai.

Ingredients (Ajza)

  • 500g Chicken
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice, washed and soaked for 30 minutes
  • 2 medium Onions, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices (2 Bay Leaves, 4-5 Cloves, 1 Cinnamon Stick)
  • 4 cups Water or Chicken Broth (Yakhni)
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps (Tarkeeb)

  1. Ek degchi mein oil garam karein aur piyaz ko golden brown hone tak fry karein.
  2. Adrak-lehsun paste aur saabit garam masalay daal kar ek minute ke liye bhunein.
  3. Chicken shamil karein aur rang tabdeel hone tak fry karein.
  4. Dahi daal kar mazeed 2-3 minute tak pakayein.
  5. Ab pani ya yakhni aur namak daal kar ubaal anay dein.
  6. Jab pani ubalne lage to us mein bhigoye hue chawal (pani nikal kar) shamil karein.
  7. Achi tarah mix karein aur tez aanch par pakayein jab tak pani khushk hone ke qareeb na ajaye.
  8. Ab aanch bilkul halki kar ke degchi ko dhak dein aur 15 minute ke liye dum par rakhein.
  9. Dum ke baad, chawalon ko fork ki madad se ahista se mix karein aur raita ke sath serve karein.
