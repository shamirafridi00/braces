Biryani

Description

Biryani ek bohat hi laziz aur mashoor South Asian dish hai. Yeh chawal, masalay, aur gosht (ya sabziyon) se mil kar banti hai. Har elaqay ki apni khas Biryani tarkeeb hoti hai.

Ingredients (Ajza)

  • 500g Chicken/Mutton
  • 2 cups Basmati Rice
  • 3 Onions, sliced
  • 2 Tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Biryani Masala
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Salt to taste

Steps (Tarkeeb)

  1. Chawal ko dho kar 30 minute ke liye bhigo dein.
  2. Piyaz ko golden brown fry karein.
  3. Ek bartan mein gosht, dahi, adrak-lehsun paste aur Biryani masala daal kar pakayein.
  4. Jab gosht gal jaye to us mein tamatar daal kar bhoon lein.
  5. Alag bartan mein chawal ko namak daal kar ubaal lein.
  6. Ab gosht aur chawal ki teh (layers) lagayein.
  7. Ise halki aanch par 15-20 minute ke liye dum dein.
  8. Garam garam serve karein.
