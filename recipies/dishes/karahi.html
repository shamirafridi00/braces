Chicken Karahi

Description

Chicken Karahi ek bohat hi mashoor aur jaldi banne wali Pakistani dish hai. Ise khas taur par ek "Karahi" (wok jaisi degchi) mein pakaya jata hai. Iska zaiqa tamatar, adrak aur hari mirchon se bhara hota hai.

Ingredients (Ajza)

  • 500g Chicken, chotay pieces mein
  • 4-5 large Tomatoes, chopped
  • 3-4 Green Chilies, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Black Pepper
  • Fresh Coriander (Dhaniya) aur Julienned Ginger (Adrak) garnish ke liye
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • Salt to taste

Steps (Tarkeeb)

  1. Ek karahi mein oil garam karein.
  2. Chicken aur adrak-lehsun paste daal kar itna bhunein ke chicken ka rang tabdeel ho jaye.
  3. Ab kate hue tamatar shamil karein aur dhak kar halki aanch par pakayein jab tak tamatar naram na ho jayein.
  4. Tamatar gal jayein to aanch tez kar ke dahi aur tamam masalay (namak ke sath) daal dein.
  5. Achi tarah bhunai karein jab tak oil alag na ho jaye.
  6. Ab kati hui hari mirchein shamil karein aur 2-3 minute mazeed pakayein.
  7. Aakhir mein taza dhaniya aur adrak daal kar garnish karein.
  8. Garam naan ya roti ke sath serve karein.
